**Q: How do I book a delivery?**
A: You can book a delivery through:
- Our mobile app (iOS/Android)
- Our website at [website URL]
- Phone call to our customer service line
- WhatsApp booking service

**Q: What information do I need to provide when booking?**
A: You'll need to provide:
- Pickup address and contact details

- Package description and approximate weight
- Preferred delivery time
- Special handling instructions (if any)

**Q: Can I schedule deliveries in advance?**
A: Yes, you can schedule deliveries up to 7 days in advance. Same-day and next-day deliveries are also available.

**Q: What are your delivery time options?**
A: We offer:
- Express delivery (1-3 hours)

**Q: How is delivery pricing calculated?**
A: Pricing is based on:
- Distance between pickup and delivery locations
- Package size and weight
- Delivery speed (express vs. standard)
- Special handling requirements

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept:
- Debit/Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve)
- Bank transfers
- Mobile money (Paystack, Flutterwave)
- Cash payment (for select services)
- Corporate accounts with invoicing

**Q: Can I get a refund if my delivery is cancelled?**
A: Yes, refunds are processed according to our refund policy. Cancellations made within 30 minutes of booking receive full refunds.

## **DELIVERY PROCESS**

**Q: How do I track my delivery?**
A: You can track your delivery through:
- Real-time tracking in our app
- SMS updates
- WhatsApp notifications
- Phone call updates

**Q: What happens if no one is available to receive the delivery?**
A: Our delivery partner will:
- Attempt to contact the recipient
- Wait for up to 10 minutes at the delivery location
- If unsuccessful, contact the sender for instructions
- Arrange a re-delivery or return to sender

**Q: What if my package is damaged during delivery?**
A: Report damaged packages immediately through our app or customer service. We investigate all damage claims and provide compensation according to our damage policy.

**Q: How do you ensure package security?**
A: We ensure security through:
